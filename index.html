Aalto Dictionary of Machine Learning

Welcome to the Aalto Dictionary of Machine Learning — a multilingual, open-access glossary of core machine learning and artificial intelligence terms. Designed for students, researchers, and educators alike, this resource offers precise definitions, examples, and visual explanations of ML concepts.

📘 Access the Dictionary

  • English PDF
  • Translations (Spanish, German, French, Greek, Finnish)

🔍 Features

  • Concise and rigorous definitions
  • Mathematical notation and formalism
  • Multilingual support
  • Free to use, adapt, and cite
